
Commands available:

list
	Description: Lists movie_info or director_info according the specified parameters.

	Options:
		* movietitle=<movie_title>|director=<director_name>|
		genre=<genre>|year=<year> [default: none]
		Description: Specifies a movie or director info within which to limit the
		results


plot
	Description: Plots average score and budget ratio according to year/genre.
	Only companies that sell at least 4 different kinds of bars
	are listed in results.

	Options:
		* year=<year>|genre=<genre> [default: none]
		Description: Specifies a country or region within which to limit the
		results.
